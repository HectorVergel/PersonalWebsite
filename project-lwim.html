About the game


Look What I've made is a metroidvania/RPG game in which
you'll have to escape from an underground prision by recovering
all your abilities and defeating challenging places and enemies.


In the game, you can choose between two characters,
two different ways to approach the escape.
Upgrade your skills as you want and make the perfect build for your character.

My contribution


In this project I've worked as a game programmer even though
I ended doing shaders and particles as it was a small team.

The main features I've worked on were; enemies behaviour, meaning combat, health and AI,
and the player movement and some player attacks.

It was my first big project and thanks to it I learned a lot about Unity engine
and improved my game programming skills.
