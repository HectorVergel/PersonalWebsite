About the game


"Un trago más"(One more drink) is a simulation game
in which you will have to make extravagant cocktails to extravagant customers.
Your hand and the physics will be your worst nightmare.

My contribution


This was a game made for the Indie Spain Jam, it was made in one week.
I've mainly worked in the customers behaviour and
some other features like the alcohol bottle dispenser.
The project was all made in Unity.
